Advocate Vraj Raval

Expert Criminal Defence Lawyer in Ahmedabad

Committed to Defending Your Rights

Advocate Vraj Raval is a dedicated criminal defence lawyer based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. With a steadfast commitment to justice, he provides robust legal representation to clients facing complex criminal charges.

He graduated with Distinction in his LLB from the prestigious GLS University, Ahmedabad, and is a registered member of the Bar Council of Gujarat.

His mission is to ensure that every client's rights are protected at every stage of the legal process, offering clear, strategic, and effective legal counsel.

Specialized in Criminal Law

Providing expert legal services across all areas of criminal defence.

Bail Applications

Expert handling of Anticipatory Bail, Regular Bail, and appeals for bail cancellation.

Criminal Trials

Strategic representation in trial proceedings before Magistrate Courts and Sessions Courts.

Appeals & Revisions

Filing and arguing Criminal Appeals and Revisions before the High Court and Sessions Court.

White-Collar Crime

Defence for cases related to fraud, embezzlement, and other economic offences.

Cheque Bounce (NI Act)

Handling matters under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act.

Cyber Crime Defence

Representation in cases related to data theft, hacking, and other IT Act offences.
